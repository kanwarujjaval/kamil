FAQ

When is the site going live?
We are launching in late spring of 2015.

Do I have to sign my life away?
Unlike most subscription based web sites, you cancel your account with the click of a button whenever you feel like it.

Is the platform free?
Yes, the platform is free to use for artists. To create a fan account, you must subscribe to your first artist.

How do I get paid?>
Your dashboard will allow you to track your subscribers and earnings. Whenever you wish to cash out, you can select to receive payment via PayPal, direct deposit or check.

What is the Split?
70% for you and 30% for our team. If you have more than 100,000 fans, shoot us a message and we will renegotiate.

How much does it cost to subscribe?
Currently, fans pay $4.99 per artist.

Why should I pay the monthly subscription fee?
The music industry is constantly changing and the truth is, its not that easy for artists to make money anymore. By contributing to their monthly income, you will be helping your favorite artists to create new music and play more shows. In return, you will receive the exclusive access that you deserve.

We are a label with multiple artists, can we sign up?
The more the merrier! Reach out to us using the contact form and we help get you started.
